Paneer Bhurji

Description:

A quick and delicious scramble of crumbled paneer cooked with onions, tomatoes, and mild spices, ideal for breakfast or a light dinner.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer: 200g (crumbled)
  • Onion: 1 (chopped)
  • Tomato: 1 (chopped)
  • Green peas (optional): 1/4 cup
  • Green chilies: 1 (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1/2 tsp
  • Turmeric: 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin seeds: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil: 1 tsp
  • Coriander leaves: for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Saut√© onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are mushy.
  4. Add crumbled paneer and green peas. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with roti or bread.
