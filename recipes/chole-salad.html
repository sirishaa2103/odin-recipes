Chole Salad

Description:

A refreshing and healthy salad featuring boiled chickpeas tossed with fresh veggies, lemon juice, and chat masala, great as a snack or side dish.

Ingredients:

  • Boiled chickpeas: 1 cup
  • Cucumber: 1 (chopped)
  • Tomato: 1 (chopped)
  • Onion: 1 (chopped)
  • Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies: 1 (optional)
  • Chat masala: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves: for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine boiled chickpeas, cucumber, tomato, onion, and green chilies.
  2. Add lemon juice, chat masala, and salt. Toss everything well.
  3. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  4. Serve as a snack or side dish.
